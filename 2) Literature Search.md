# Literature Search

Literature was collected from electronic databases including PUBMED/MEDLINE, Web of Science, PsychInfo, SCOPUS, and CENTRAL. There were no search date restrictions. 
Other methods for identifying relevant research included contacting authors/experts and reference list checking. The full search strings can be found in the table below.

### Inclusion Criteria
- Studies on patients of all ages with neurological and/or psychiatric disorders
- Studies involving tACS reporting at least one of the following outcomes: Motor function, Cognition, Mental Health, Neuroimaging
- Studies conducted in a clinical setting or at home in any country
- Both randomized and non-randomized studies

### Exclusion Criteria
- Participants without a clinical diagnosis
- Studies of non-tACS neuromodulation techniques (e.g. only tDCS)
- Animal or in-vitro studies
- Case reports, narrative reviews, editorials, letters, protocols without original data, dissertations
- Conference abstracts or posters for which neither full-text or sufficient outcome data can be obtained
- Full text not available in English or German

### The literature search was conducted in 2 phases: 

1) **Abstract screening:** In this initial stage, reviewers examined the titles and abstracts of all identified articles to quickly determine their potential relevance. Studies that clearly did not meet the inclusion criteria were excluded.

2) **Full-text screening:** Articles that passed the abstract screening were then assessed in full. Reviewers examined the entire text to determine whether each study met all inclusion criteria and was suitable for the review.

Screening was performed independently on Rayyan by three blinded reviewers. Any disagreements were discussed and resolved through consensus.
The full systematic review is still ongoing and therefore, the total number of studies included and excluded cannot be disclosed. However, for this practical project, the following number of studies have been identified. 

- **MoCA:** 3 studies
- **ADAS-Cog:** 4 studies
- **RAVLT-IR:** 6 studies
- **RAVLT-DR:** 5 studies
